Shawn Michael Gallagher is a computer science undergraduate in Pennsylvania. He has significant customer service experience and is acquainted with computer technologies like full-stack development and digital design. He is seeking a part-time position or internship for the spring/summer.

Education

Gallagher attended Parkland High School from September 2018 to June 2022 and graduated with a 3.9 GPA, earning High Honor Roll every semester. Starting in 2022, he attended the Honors College at Bloomsburg University (Computer Science, BS) for two semesters before transferring to Pennsylvania Western University. He maintains a 3.52 GPA and is expected to graduate by Spring 2026.

Work experience

  • From August 2020 to February 2022, Gallagher worked as a cashier and shift leader at YoFresh Yogurt Café[1] in Allentown, Pennsylvania. He would open, close, and oversee the store as a stand-in manager. He was also trained to disassemble, clean, and reassemble the store's machinery for producing frozen yogurt and rolled ice cream. He also occasionally coordinated coupon/discount deals with other businesses in the shopping center.

He would greet customers and bring them to their tables. The job also included bussing tables, restocking silverware, and petting customers' dogs on the patio.

The position involved scanning local historical documents such as scrapbooks, magazines, and architectural blueprints to upload to an online database. He would often write Excel macros and image-processing scripts.

After training as a barback for two months, Gallagher worked on weekends mixing and serving cocktail orders from a bar adjacent to a dance floor. The position also included coordinating time slots with music performers/DJs and placing orders for supplies/ingredients. By the end of camp, students were able to use laptops and iPads proficiently, communicate basic coding concepts, and show off a variety of handcrafted games and animations.
